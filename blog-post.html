REVIEW: Dairy & Frozen: Egg Tart

July 21, 2025

Why I Bought It

I spotted these Portuguese-style egg tarts in the frozen section. They’re usually $7.19 each, but on this day, they were on sale—two for just $9.70! A deal I couldn’t pass up.

Packaging & Preparation

The packaging is clear and reassuring, with a photo that shows exactly what you’re getting. Just heat them up in the oven or air fryer—super simple and no hassle.

Taste Test

Surprisingly delicious! The pastry is crispy and flaky, and the custard is smooth with just the right amount of sweetness. It’s light, with a subtle vanilla flavor that makes it perfect for snacking.

Best Enjoyed When…

  • During a cozy afternoon tea
  • When guests drop by unexpectedly
  • You’re just craving something sweet

The quality really makes you think: “Wait, this was frozen?”

They do sell fresh-baked egg tarts in the bakery section at T&T, and while those are a bit bigger, the frozen ones taste just as good. Definitely worth keeping in the freezer for a treat anytime.
